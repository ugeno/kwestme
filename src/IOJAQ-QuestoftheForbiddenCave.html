Quest of the Forbidden Cave

When you were little you were always told not to go to the "Forbidden Cave" because there were monsters and dangers.
You weren't told exactly were this cave was but you were also told not to go past the tree line on the far horizon of the village,
so you thought that was were the cave was.
Past the horizon...

12 years later.
Wondering through the town you look out at the horizon and ponder about what lies past it, you decided you shall go explore it.
Alone with no-one the bother you, so you go home and gather suplies; clothes, food, a homemade sling-shot and the dagger you got for your 16th birthday.
You saddled your horse and rode towards the tree line, carefree with your only thoughts being to get past the trees.
You get there and their are two paths but on both of them their is a rotting, mossy wooden sign saying,
'DANGER! DO NOT ENTER'
But this was what everyone had always told you you looked at both the paths. Both leading deep into the woods.
Both leading to the forbidden cave...
